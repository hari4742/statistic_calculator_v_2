How to Use this Calculator?

  • Click on "Calculate →" under the section which you want to calculate.
  • Now, Click on 👇 to expand the section based on type of data you want to calculate.
    • In Discrete Data Section:
    • Enter numbers seperated by ','
    • Don't give spaces any where.
    • Ex: "1,4,25,2,2"
    • In Grouped Data section:
    • Select the type of class and click on OK.
    • Enter Lower and Upper boundary values and number of rows you want. Then, Click on Make table.
    • Fill the Frequency values and Click on Calculate.
  • If the Calculation has Exclusive and Inclusive sections seperatly, then skip the first step from the above Grouped Data section and proceed with step 2 and step 3.
